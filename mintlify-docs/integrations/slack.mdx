---
title: "Slack"
description: "Use Slack as a destination for meeting follow-through and team communication."
---

Slack is useful when meeting outcomes need to land where the team already communicates.

## Common uses

- post summaries to the right channel
- share action items with the team
- route follow-through after the meeting

## Recommended rollout

Start with approval-based posting so users confirm destination and wording before messages land in shared channels.
