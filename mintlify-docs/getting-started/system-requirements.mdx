Shadow is designed around a Mac desktop workflow.

The latest desktop installer is available here: [Download Shadow for Mac](https://github.com/Converse-AI-Inc/Shadow/releases/latest/download/Shadow.dmg).

## Required

- A Mac desktop or laptop
- A Shadow account
- Access to the meeting tools your team uses
- Permission to grant local Mac privacy permissions

## Core software and access expectations

- Users need access to the calendar and meeting tools they actually use
- Users should know whether they are signing in with Google or Microsoft
- Enterprise users should know whether they are an admin or a member

## Recommended setup by user type

### Enterprise admin using Microsoft, Outlook, Teams, and Salesforce

- Microsoft work account
- Outlook calendar access
- Microsoft Teams meeting links on calendar events
- Salesforce access for the admin who will connect it

### Enterprise member using Microsoft, Outlook, and Teams

- invited work email address
- access to the same Microsoft account used for work calendar
- Outlook calendar events with valid Teams links

### Individual free or pro user, typically with Google

- Google account
- Google Calendar access
- supported meeting links on calendar events where applicable

## Why the Mac permissions matter

Shadow uses Mac permissions to support live meeting workflows:

- `Accessibility`: detect meeting launches and help with app-level coordination
- `Microphone`: capture the user's side of the conversation
- `System Audio`: capture meeting participant audio

Without these permissions, the setup completes only partially and live meeting workflows may fail.

## Supported meeting platforms

Shadow currently recognizes supported meeting URLs and live workflows for:

- Zoom
- Microsoft Teams
- Google Meet
- Webex

<Note>
  Shadow runs natively on the Mac rather than joining the meeting as a separate bot participant.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Teams does not need a separate standalone integration for meeting detection. Salesforce is not a universal requirement; it is primarily relevant for enterprise admin CRM workflows.
</Tip>
