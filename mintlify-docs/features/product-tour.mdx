Shadow is best understood as one meeting workflow across three stages. The workflow is consistent, but the setup path depends on whether the user is an enterprise admin, enterprise member, or individual user.

## Before you start the product tour

Keep these setup rules in mind:

- Google users typically grant calendar access during Google sign-in
- Microsoft users grant calendar access during Microsoft sign-in
- Teams does not require a separate standalone integration to detect meetings
- Salesforce is relevant primarily for enterprise admins in post-meeting CRM workflows

## 1. Before the meeting

- dashboard for upcoming calls
- prep page for agenda, context, and sources
- attendee and company brief

## 2. During the meeting

- live transcript
- live cards and nudges
- approvals and clarifications
- overlay support

## 3. After the meeting

- summary and recap
- action items and follow-through
- source usage
- CRM proposals such as Salesforce write-back when the relevant integration is connected
