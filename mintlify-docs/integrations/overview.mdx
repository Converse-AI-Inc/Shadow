Shadow connects to the tools where meetings are scheduled, work gets routed, and follow-through lands.

## Current customer-facing integration categories

### Calendar and communication

- Outlook Calendar
- Outlook Mail
- Gmail
- Google Calendar
- Slack

### Knowledge and documentation

- Notion
- Google Docs

### Work tracking

- Jira
- Linear

### CRM

- Salesforce

## How to think about integrations

- calendar integrations power meeting discovery and scheduling context
- email integrations power draft-based follow-up
- knowledge integrations help Shadow use and route written context
- work tracking integrations turn outcomes into tracked work
- CRM integrations turn meetings into pipeline updates
