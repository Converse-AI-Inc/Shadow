Shadow supports Microsoft Teams as a meeting platform in the desktop workflow.

## Recommended setup

For Teams users, the strongest setup is:

- Outlook Calendar connected
- Outlook Mail connected
- Teams meeting links present on the calendar event
- required Mac permissions granted

## What this enables

- upcoming Teams meetings on the dashboard
- prep and review flows around scheduled meetings
- live meeting assistance during the call

## Before the first Teams meeting

1. Connect Outlook Calendar.
2. Confirm the event includes the Teams join link.
3. Complete Mac permissions.
4. Open the meeting from Shadow before the call begins.

## Common issue

If Teams meetings are not appearing correctly, the first thing to verify is usually the Outlook calendar integration and the event join link, not the Teams app itself.
