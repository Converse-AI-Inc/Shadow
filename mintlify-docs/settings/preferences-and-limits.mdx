title: "Settings, Preferences, and Limits"
description: "How users should think about settings, usage, and common operational constraints."
---

Shadow includes configuration and usage boundaries that affect daily operation.

## Areas users should understand

- integrations and reconnect state
- preferences that affect meeting behavior
- feature and usage limits on the current plan
- source count limits
- any meeting usage limits that apply to the account

## Practical guidance

- Check integrations first when a workflow fails unexpectedly.
- Keep user expectations clear around what is live, what is draft-based, and what requires approval.
- Review limits before broad rollout if the team plans high meeting volume.
