## Why can't Shadow start properly during onboarding?

The most common cause is missing Mac permissions. Check Accessibility, Microphone, and System Audio first.

## Why are my Teams meetings not showing correctly?

Usually this means Outlook Calendar is not connected correctly or the calendar event is missing a valid Teams join link.

## Why does Shadow ask me to reconnect Outlook or Salesforce?

OAuth tokens can expire or be revoked. Use the reconnect action from the Integrations page and complete the browser flow again.

## Does Shadow send emails automatically?

Shadow should be explained as a draft-first system for email workflows. Users review and send the draft themselves.

## Why don't I see Salesforce updates after every meeting?

Salesforce proposals depend on both the integration state and whether the meeting produced enough CRM-relevant context to generate a proposal.

## What should I check first if live meeting help is not appearing?

1. confirm the required Mac permissions
2. confirm the meeting was opened through the supported workflow
3. confirm the meeting platform is one of the supported platforms
4. confirm the user is in the correct live meeting view
