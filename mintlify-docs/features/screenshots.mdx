This page helps users and customer champions understand what Shadow looks like before rollout.

The screenshots below map to the main product workflow:

- context before the meeting
- briefing and agenda prep
- live in-meeting assistance
- real-time nudges
- review and follow-through after the call

## Connected context before the meeting

![Connected deal context](/images/connected-deal-context.png)

Shadow brings together the account and meeting context before the call starts.

## Briefing before the call

![Deal brief before the call](/images/deal-brief-before-call.png)

The prep workflow helps users review the meeting, refine the agenda, and align on the goal.

## Live answers during the conversation

![Live answers to tough questions](/images/live-answers-tough-questions.png)

The live meeting assistant surfaces relevant guidance while the discussion is still happening.

## Real-time nudges

![Realtime nudges that move the deal](/images/realtime-nudges-move-deal.png)

Shadow can surface prompts and meeting guidance that help the user stay sharp in the moment.

## Automatic recap and CRM-oriented follow-through

![Automatic recap and CRM updates](/images/automatic-recap-crm-updates.png)

After the meeting, Shadow turns the call into reviewable outputs that the user can act on.
