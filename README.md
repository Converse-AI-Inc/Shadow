## What is Shadow?

Shadow sits in the background during your meetings and works so you don't have to.

- **Live transcription** — real-time, accurate transcripts as the meeting happens
- **Wingman** — in-meeting assistant that surfaces context, talking points, and agenda coverage on the fly
- **Meeting review** — auto-generated summaries, action items, and highlights after every call
- **Source library** — bring in documents, notes, and past meetings so Shadow always has the right context
- **Calendar sync** — works with Google Calendar and Microsoft Calendar to know your schedule

No manual setup per meeting. Shadow detects your calls automatically.

## Installation

1. Download `Shadow-x.x.x.dmg` from the [latest release](https://github.com/Converse-AI-Inc/Shadow/releases/latest)
2. Open the `.dmg` and drag **Shadow** into `/Applications`
3. Launch Shadow and sign in
4. Grant screen recording and microphone permissions when prompted

---

## Updating

Shadow checks for updates automatically. When a new version is available you'll see an **Update Now** prompt in **Settings → Billing & Invoice**. Click it to download and install in the background — no manual DMG download needed.
