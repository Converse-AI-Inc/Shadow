---
title: "Salesforce"
description: "Use Salesforce with Shadow for post-meeting CRM proposals and controlled write-back."
---

Salesforce is currently a post-meeting integration in Shadow.

## What users see

On the meeting review page, Shadow can surface Salesforce proposals such as:

- lead creation
- contact creation
- opportunity creation or updates

## Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Salesforce">
    Finish the OAuth flow from the Integrations page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run the meeting">
    Use Shadow in the normal prep, live, and review flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the Salesforce section">
    Open the meeting review page and check the proposals generated for the meeting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Push approved records">
    Push only the records the user has reviewed and confirmed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Important expectation

Shadow does not require customers to accept blind CRM writes. The approval step is the safer operating model and should be explained clearly during onboarding.
